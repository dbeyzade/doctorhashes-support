Privacy Policy for DoctorHashes

Last updated: October 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to DoctorHashes. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application available on iOS App Store and Google Play Store.

2. Information We Collect

DoctorHashes does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All cryptographic operations, hash calculations, and encryption/decryption processes are performed entirely locally on your device.

3. Data Processing

All data you input into the app (including text, files, and cryptographic operations) is:

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never sent to external servers
  • Never stored permanently unless you choose to save results locally
  • Never shared with any third parties

4. Offline Functionality

DoctorHashes operates completely offline. No internet connection is required or used for any cryptographic operations. Your privacy is protected by design.

5. Premium Features & In-App Purchases

If you purchase premium features through in-app purchases:

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store
  • We do not receive or store your payment information
  • Transaction data is governed by Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies
  • We only receive a confirmation of purchase status from the app stores

6. Third-Party Services

DoctorHashes does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics or tracking services
  • No advertising networks
  • No cloud storage services
  • No social media integrations

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for users who understand cryptographic concepts and is not targeted at children.

8. Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, your information is as secure as your device itself. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using device encryption features
  • Setting up device passcode/biometric locks

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to:

  • Request access to
  • Request deletion of
  • Request correction of
  • Request portability of

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting an in-app notification for significant changes

11. Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) - EU
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) - USA
  • Google Play Families Policy
  • Apple App Store Guidelines
