Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 15, 2025

1. Introduction

DoctorHashes is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our cryptographic hash generator and analyzer application.

2. Data Collection

Local Processing: All hash generation and analysis is performed locally on your device. We do not upload or transmit your files or text inputs to any server.

  • Basic app usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Device information (for compatibility)
  • Crash reports (if you opt-in)

What We Don't Collect:

  • Your files or text inputs
  • Generated hash values
  • Personal identification information
  • Location data

3. Data Usage

The minimal data we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure compatibility across devices
  • Fix bugs and crashes

4. Data Security

Your privacy is our priority. All processing happens locally on your device. We use industry-standard security measures to protect any data that is collected. All data is encrypted and stored on secure servers.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with third parties. The app may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting (with your consent), but these services receive only anonymized data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any data we have collected
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics and crash reporting

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes.
